SHIF Compliance Reminders

The Social Health Insurance Fund (SHIF) is mandatory for all Kenyan residents earning a salary. Employers and employees must ensure timely contributions to remain compliant and access health benefits.

  • Contribution due date: 9th of the month following the payroll month
  • All residents and salaried employees are required to contribute
  • Late payment attracts a 2% penalty per month on the outstanding contribution
  • Registration is mandatory through the official SHIF portal
  • Employers must remit contributions on behalf of employees to avoid penalties
  • SHIF compliance ensures eligibility for government health coverage and benefits

SHIF Benefits Overview

  • Comprehensive inpatient and outpatient medical coverage
  • Maternal and child healthcare services
  • Management support for chronic diseases such as diabetes and hypertension
  • Emergency and referral medical services
  • Access to approved public and private hospitals across Kenya
  • Financial protection against high medical costs
